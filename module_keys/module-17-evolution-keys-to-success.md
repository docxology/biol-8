# Module 17: Evolution — Keys to Success

## Learning Objectives

1. **Explain Natural Selection**: Describe how variations, inheritance, and Differential Reproductive Success lead to evolutionary change.
2. **Patterns of Evolution**: Contrast **convergent evolution**, **divergent evolution**, and **coevolution**.
3. **Evidence for Common Descent**: Identify biological and physical evidence, including fossils, comparative anatomy, and DNA sequencing.
4. **Speciation and Adaptation**: Explain the process of species formation and the importance of adaptation in changing environments.
5. **Timeline of Life**: Understand the major milestones in the history of life on Earth and their evolutionary significance.

## Study Tips
- Remember that natural selection acts on **individuals**, but evolution occurs in **populations**.
- Focus on the role of genetic variation as the "raw material" for evolution.
- Review the concept of "common ancestry" and its relationship to phylogeny.
