# Module 16: Ecology — Keys to Success

## Learning Objectives

1. **Define Ecology**: Understand the levels of biological organization from individual to biosphere.
2. **Population Dynamics**: Describe population growth models (exponential and logistic) and factors affecting population size.
3. **Community Interactions**: Identify and explain different types of species interactions (competition, predation, symbiosis).
4. **Ecosystem Energy Flow**: Diagram energy flow through trophic levels and the importance of producers and decomposers.
5. **Biomes and Biosphere**: Describe the major terrestrial and aquatic biomes and the global impact of human activity.

## Study Tips
- Focus on the differences between population and community ecology.
- Practice drawing a food web for a local ecosystem.
- Review the concept of carrying capacity and its relevance to human population growth.
