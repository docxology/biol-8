## Overview

This lab explores the mechanisms of **evolution**, including natural selection, adaptation, and the shared characters that provide evidence for common descent. You will model the process of natural selection and compare anatomical structures across different species.

---

## Learning Objectives

- Model the process of natural selection using simulation or physical modeling.
- Differentiate between homologous and analogous structures.
- Analyze fossil evidence to determine evolutionary trends.
- Use DNA sequences or protein comparisons to build a simple phylogenetic tree.

---

## Materials (per station)

- Selection boxes or simulation software
- Skeletal models or comparative anatomy images
- Fossil specimens or replicas
- DNA sequence data sets
