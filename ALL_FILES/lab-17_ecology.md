## Overview

This lab introduces the study of **ecology**, the branch of biology that deals with the relations of organisms to one another and to their physical surroundings. You will explore population growth models, community interactions, and the impact of environmental factors on biodiversity.

---

## Learning Objectives

- Define the levels of biological organization from individual to biosphere.
- Model population growth using exponential and logistic data.
- Identify and describe symbiotic relationships within a local ecosystem.
- Analyze the flow of energy through a simplified food web.
