Module 2 Study Guide: Cell Biology

Key Terms

Cell: Basic unit of life
Organelle: Specialized structure within a cell
Prokaryote: Cell without membrane-bound nucleus
Eukaryote: Cell with membrane-bound nucleus
Nucleus: Organelle containing DNA
Mitochondria: Organelle producing ATP
Ribosome: Site of protein synthesis
Diffusion: Passive movement of molecules
Osmosis: Diffusion of water
Active Transport: Energy-requiring movement

Organelle Functions

| Organelle | Function |
|-----------|----------|
| Nucleus | Contains DNA, controls cell |
| Mitochondria | Produces energy (ATP) |
| Ribosomes | Synthesize proteins |
| ER | Protein and lipid processing |
| Golgi | Modifies and packages proteins |
| Lysosomes | Digest cellular waste |
