StructureRole (survey level)
DendritesReceive signals from other cells; graded potentials toward soma.
Axon hillock / initial segmentIntegrates input; initiates action potential if threshold reached.
Myelin (Schwann / oligodendrocyte)Insulates axon; saltatory conduction at nodes of Ranvier.
SynapsePresynaptic terminal → cleft → postsynaptic membrane; chemical or electrical.
