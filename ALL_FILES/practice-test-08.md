A) The thick and thin filaments get shorter

B) Serotonin

### Muscle Mechanics

A) One muscle fiber and all the nerves that connect to it
B) One motor neuron and all the muscle fibers it controls

A) A single twitch
B) Summation
C) Tetanus
D) Fatigue

A) The muscle generates force but does not change length
B) The muscle changes length and moves a load

**16.** Muscle fatigue is most directly caused by:
A) A buildup of new muscle fibers
B) Depletion of ATP and accumulation of metabolic byproducts
C) Excess calcium in the blood
